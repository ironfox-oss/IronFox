And so, the Mole had fallen. As the Beast had already lost its way, the believers began to lose faith, and the world was left in darkness. As hope was almost lost, a Fox created a spark. Drawn to its light, a Phoenix bonded with the Fox to form a special coat of Iron. With its newfound strength, the creature rose to avenge the Mole and the Beast that fell before it.
